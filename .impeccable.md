# Impeccable Design Context

Synthesized from CONTEXT.md, globals.css, and the shipped UI (2026-07-20). Refine freely — this file is the source of truth for design skills.

## Design Context

### Users
Households (often two partners, one shared login) building a financial life together — people who would rather **not** stare at numbers. They check in on phones between errands; the Sunday-scan test (read the coming two weeks in under a minute) defines success. Jobs: see where money went in the plan's own language, correct a miscategorized transaction in seconds, feel encouraged rather than judged.

### Brand Personality
Warm, honest, encouraging. The Honesty Rule governs tone: never say "fine" when it isn't — say "fixable, here's how." Plain language over finance jargon; the app speaks the household's plan vocabulary (CSP buckets, Money Dials), never a third taxonomy. Three words: **warm, candid, calm**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm paper-and-ink domesticity: cream backgrounds (#FAF7F2), warm browns for text, a single gold accent (#C4A265) with muted category colors (sage, slate-blue, terracotta, plum). Playfair Display for headings, DM Sans for body. Light theme only, by design. Motion is quiet: fade-up entrances, ease-out, full reduced-motion support. Anti-references: fintech dashboards with dark mode + neon, gamified budgeting apps, shame-driven red overspend warnings.

### Design Principles
1. **Speak the plan's language** — every label comes from the household's own CSP/Money Dial vocabulary.
2. **Honesty without shame** — show the true state, pair it with one next action, never hide or soften.
3. **One glance, one action** — surfaces lead with a single number or sentence and at most one thing to do.
4. **The feed drafts, the human ratifies** — automation proposes; people confirm with minimal taps.
5. **Calm by default** — quiet motion, warm neutrals, gold reserved for what matters.
